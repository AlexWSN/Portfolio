Professional Background

Hello, my name is Alexandra. Since 2019, I have been working as a supervisor in parental education for the HoltIS Association. All the projects I have been involved in have been carried out in collaboration with UNICEF. Among these, one of the key initiatives I contributed to was the establishment of HoltIS youth clubs in 140 schools nationwide, supporting both middle and high school students.

I am passionate about painting, music, technology and always open to new discoveries. I find motivation in challenges that promote continuous learning and personal growth.

I strongly value fairplay and believe in both offering and receiving respect. For me, mutual respect, integrity, and collaboration are essential in creating meaningful connections and a positive environment.
